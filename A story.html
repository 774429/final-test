A quick story about us

        We are a local charity that believes no child should go hungry, and the best way to
prevent that from happining is to take action in the comunity.
We saw a need in our comunity and were convicted by it so we made the change.
So whoever you are if you feel the conviction and have the means to give, please do.
we are not a charity focused on makeing a name for ourselves.
We do not waste monney paying billionare board members to advise what sad tv advertizements to play next.
We are a group acting united in good conscience to combat the pain of hunger because
we are all human and should sympathise.
